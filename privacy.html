Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dealsmartusa1 ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and our integration with TikTok's Content Posting API and Login Kit.

1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to visitors of our website and to the TikTok account holder(s) who authorize our application to publish content on their behalf via TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API.

2. Information we collect

  • TikTok account authorization data: when you connect your TikTok account to our application, we receive an access token and refresh token from TikTok that allow us to publish content on your behalf. We do not receive your TikTok password.
  • Basic profile information: if granted, we may receive your TikTok open ID and basic profile information (such as display name) solely to confirm which account is connected.
  • Content data: the images, descriptions, and prices we publish, which are sourced from our own Telegram channel, not from end users.
  • Website usage data: standard, non-identifying technical data such as browser type, may be collected automatically when you visit our website.

3. How we use this information

  • To authenticate and maintain the connection between our application and the authorized TikTok account.
  • To publish photo posts (image, description, and price) to the authorized TikTok account via the Content Posting API.
  • To troubleshoot and maintain the reliability of the posting integration.

We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok account data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data retention

Access and refresh tokens are stored only for as long as needed to maintain the active connection to TikTok. If you revoke our application's access through TikTok's settings, we delete the associated tokens from our systems.

5. Data security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored tokens and account data from unauthorized access, including restricting access to systems that handle this data.

6. Your rights and choices

You may revoke our application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app permissions settings. You may also contact us to request deletion of any data we hold about your connected account.

7. Third-party services

Our integration relies on TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API. Your interaction with TikTok itself is governed by TikTok's own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

8. Children's privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy, or requests to access or delete your data, can be directed to us at goodealz.vik@gmail.com.
